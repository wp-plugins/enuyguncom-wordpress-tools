This is the official plugin that collect Enuygun.com's affiliate and api tools

Enuygun.com is the leading services comparison site in Turkey. We operate in 4 verticals: Money (Loans & Mortgages), Insurance (Auto insurance), Home Services (ADSL & GSM tariffs) and Travel (Flight tickets). 

Enuygun.com offers consumers an easy-to-use financial services comparison experience, and helps them save time and money.
